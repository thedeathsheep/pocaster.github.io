## What counts as a project

This page is for non-game work only. It covers writing, tools, experiments, and other things that are useful to keep track of separately from game projects.

## Included here

- Site tooling and small utilities.
- Creative or technical experiments that are not games.
- Long-lived personal projects that deserve their own reference page.

## Status notes

- Some projects may be active.
- Some may be archived or paused.
- I'll note the state of each project so it's easy to tell what is current.
